About

Who we are

At Elite Swimming Club, our mission is to offer exceptional coaching and training opportunities for individuals seeking to learn swimming skills and enhance water safety awareness.

  • Offering top-tier coaching and training programs for new learners
  • Providing state-of-the-art facilities and equipment to improve swimmers' skills
  • Summer Camps, Public play hours, Monthly and Yearly Memberships are available

Join us for an unforgettable summer experience at Elite Swimming Club's Summer Camp! Our camp offers young swimmers the chance to immerse themselves in the sport they love while receiving expert coaching and guidance from our experienced staff. From stroke technique to strength training, our campers will develop their skills in a fun and supportive environment.
